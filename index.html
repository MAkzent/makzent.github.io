Michael Arnold

Product Guy, Idealist, Gamer

This is my personal thinkpad to digest and reflect on my journey as a founder, product guy, and now programmer. I've studied General Management in Germany and moved to San Francisco after my graduation to join an early stage startup called NeoReach. There, I've learned a ton about startups, influencer marketing, inbound- and outbound marketing, and operations to run a team.

After 1.5y at NeoReach, I've packed my bags and went back to Berlin to turn my esports passion into my own company. I've raised money from Bitkraft, hired a small team, and started the company PatronGG to give fan communities a home. We've built our very first product with the esports team Fnatic and it's live on the Play Store and App Store.
